Welcome to our webpage. our names are John, Bryan and Jonathan and we are Computer Science Students at DSST College View High School.

In this class, we created two different 3D rings based on the Lantern Corps from DC Comics. Our main issue was making the rings printable. This is because of the large opening where your finger is meant to be placed. Despite this, we believe our end product was very successful and accurate to the rings shown in the Green Lantern DC Comic books.
